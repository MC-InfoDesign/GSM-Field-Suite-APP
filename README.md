Installing an APK
These apps aren't on the Play Store yet (beta phase). To install:

Download the .apk from the links above
On your Android phone, allow "install from unknown sources" when prompted
Open the downloaded file, install, done
Why no Play Store yet
These are still in active testing. Direct APK distribution lets me ship fixes fast and collect feedback before a public listing. If you hit a bug or have an idea, use the in-app report button, it opens a pre-filled email to contact@gsm-field-suite.com.
